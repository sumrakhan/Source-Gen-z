What Are the Uyghur Concentration Camps?

The Uyghur Muslims are a small ethnic group in China considered by the Chinese government as a regional minority within a multicultural nation. The Uyghurs are fighting for cultural unity within their nation but they are getting put into concentration camps for doing so. In the camps they are being taught to promote communism and worship the Chinese president. This act is taking away from their personal identity and religious beliefs.
