What is the Black Lives Matter Movement?

The Black Lives Matter movement is a socially organized movement against the systematic opression and injustice African Americans face on a day to day basis. This movement fights against the police brutality that causes so many innocent lives to be lost due to the fact that their skin color is different. Everyday a new story is brought to light and quickly brushed under the rug, but it is up to us to fight against this.
